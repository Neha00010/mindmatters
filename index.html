“Mental illness… occurs when our mental health is compromised or neglected for so long that it affects our ability to function in our everyday life.” --Kati Morton

Here's the fact, that More than 40% of countries lack a mental health policy and up to 60% of depressed people can recover with the correct medication and psychotherapy regimen. A billion individuals worldwide are affected by mental illness or drug addiction where mainly Womens and the poor people are more prone to have mental health problems. In nine out of 10 cases, people with mental health disorders show that stigma and prejudice have a negative influence on their life. Our website is a little step towards solving this problem by extending a helping hand to those in need. We want to reach as many individuals as possible and create a community of healthy, happy people.
